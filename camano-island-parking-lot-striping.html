Camano Island Striping FAQs

Do you provide service for HOAs on the south end of Camano?

Yes! We provide full-service striping for homeowners associations and private communities across the entire island, including the Country Club and Madrona Beach areas.

Does the island air affect the paint's lifespan?

The salt air on Camano is less abrasive than the outer islands, but we still use premium high-solids traffic paint to ensure your lines stay bright for 2-3 years even in coastal conditions.

How quickly can you give me a quote?

Since we can drive directly to Camano, we can often provide an on-site evaluation and quote within 24-48 hours of your request.
