Edmonds Striping FAQs

Can you stripe at night to avoid ferry commuter traffic?

Yes! For Edmonds properties near the ferry terminal or on Edmonds Way (SR-104), we offer night shifts to ensure your lot is dry and ready before the morning commuter rush.

Do your markings meet Edmonds Fire Department safety codes?

Absolutely. We provide safety-compliant fire lane markings, including red curb painting and "No Parking" stencils, ensuring emergency access is always clear.

Does the maritime air affect how long my striping will last?

Salt air can be more abrasive to standard paints. We recommend using our high-durability traffic paints for Edmonds waterfront properties, typically lasting 2-3 years depending on traffic volume.
