Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our warehouse and factory floor marking services in Whatcom County

What types of warehouse floor markings do you offer?

We offer a complete range of industrial floor markings including safety lanes, pedestrian walkways, forklift paths, loading zones, storage area boundaries, hazard zones, and equipment placement markings. We use OSHA-compliant colors and can customize markings to match your facility's specific needs.

What paint do you use for industrial floors?

We use high-durability industrial epoxy and traffic-grade floor paint designed to withstand heavy forklift traffic, foot traffic, and equipment movement. Our coatings are chemical-resistant and provide long-lasting visibility even in high-traffic areas.

Can you work around our production schedule?

Yes! We understand that downtime costs money. We offer flexible scheduling including nights, weekends, and phased installation to minimize disruption to your operations. We can also work section-by-section to keep parts of your facility operational during the project.

What areas do you serve for warehouse floor marking?

We provide warehouse and factory floor marking services throughout Whatcom County including Lynden, Bellingham, Ferndale, Blaine, Everson, and Nooksack. We also serve parts of Skagit, Snohomish, San Juan, and Island Counties. Contact us to confirm service availability for your location.

How much does warehouse floor marking cost?

Pricing varies based on the size of your facility, complexity of the layout, type of coatings required, and scope of work. Most warehouse floor marking projects range from $500-$5,000+ depending on size and requirements. Contact us for a free, no-obligation quote and facility assessment.
