Frequently Asked Questions - Mount Vernon Parking Lot Striping

How often should parking lots in Mount Vernon be restriped?

Due to Northwest Washington's wet climate and frequent freeze-thaw cycles, we recommend restriping every 2-3 years for most commercial properties in Mount Vernon and Skagit County. High-traffic areas like retail centers near the Cascade Mall or medical facilities may need annual restriping.

What are the ADA requirements for parking in Mount Vernon?

Washington State requires 1 accessible space for the first 25 parking spots, plus 1 additional space for every 50 spaces thereafter. All ADA spaces must be 8 feet wide with a 5-foot access aisle (or 11 feet for van-accessible spaces). We ensure full compliance with federal ADA guidelines and Washington State regulations.
