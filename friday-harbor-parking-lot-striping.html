Friday Harbor Striping FAQs

How do you manage the logistics of getting equipment to Friday Harbor?

We are experienced in island logistics. We pre-book our ferry reservations and coordinate our schedule to arrive and depart efficiently, often completing work in a single trip to save you on mobilization costs.

Does the salt air affect the lifespan of the paint?

Coastal environments are tougher on markings. We use high-solids traffic paint that offers superior resistance to fading and moisture common in the San Juan Islands.

Can you stripe at night to avoid ferry-related traffic?

Yes! For businesses near the ferry terminal or on **Spring Street**, we offer night shifts to ensure the lot is dry and ready before the first ferry arrives in the morning.
