Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our sports court striping services in Northwest Washington

How long does sports court striping last?

With proper maintenance, professional sports court striping typically lasts 1-2 years in the Pacific Northwest. Factors like foot traffic and usage, weather exposure, and pavement condition affect longevity. We use high-quality traffic paint designed to withstand Whatcom County's wet climate.

How long until the sports court can be used after striping?

Drying time depends on weather conditions. In good weather (dry, warm), lines typically dry in 30-60 minutes. We recommend waiting 1-2 hours before allowing play to ensure complete drying and maximum durability.

What areas do you serve for sports court striping?

We provide sports court striping throughout Whatcom County including Lynden, Bellingham, Ferndale, Blaine, Everson, and Nooksack. We also serve Skagit County, Snohomish County, San Juan County, and Island County. Contact us to confirm service availability for your location.

How much does sports court striping cost?

Pricing varies based on sports court size, current condition, and additional services like stenciling or ADA markings. Contact us for a free, no-obligation quote specific to your property.
