Frequently Asked Questions

What sports court lines do you paint?

We paint lines for basketball, tennis, pickleball, volleyball, badminton, futsal, and other recreational courts. We can also create custom multi-sport layouts with different colored lines for each sport.

Do you follow regulation court dimensions?

Yes, we use official regulation dimensions for all sports. Whether you need NCAA, USTA, USA Pickleball, or recreational standard dimensions, we ensure accurate measurements for proper gameplay.

How long does sports court striping take?

Most single-sport courts can be completed in one day. Multi-sport courts with multiple line colors may take 2-3 days to allow proper drying time between colors.

What type of paint do you use?

We use professional-grade athletic court paint designed for durability and visibility. Our paints are formulated for either asphalt or concrete surfaces and provide excellent adhesion and longevity.

Can you stripe both indoor and outdoor courts?

Yes, we provide striping services for both indoor gymnasium floors and outdoor asphalt or concrete courts. We use appropriate paint types for each surface to ensure optimal results.
