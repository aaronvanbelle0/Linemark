Recent Projects

A showcase of our latest work in Whatcom & Skagit Counties

Bellingham, WA

Shopping Center Fire Lane & Restripe

This busy retail center in Bellingham had faded fire lanes that were becoming a liability issue. We performed a night-shift restripe to minimize disruption to businesses. The project included 150+ stalls, 800 feet of red fire curb, and new "No Parking" stencils.

  • Night Shift Work
Lynden, WA

ADA Compliance Upgrade

We brought this older parking lot up to current 2025 standards. The job involved blacking out old, non-compliant markings and installing new 8-foot access aisles with Van-Accessible signage. We used high-visibility blue and white traffic paint to ensure clear accessibility for all patrons.

  • Handicap Stenciling
  • Blackout & Re-stripe
Ferndale, WA

Industrial Loading Zones

Safety is priority #1 for this distribution center in Ferndale. We striped new yellow hash-marked loading zones and defined forklift crossing paths. We utilized industrial-grade traffic paint designed to withstand heavy truck traffic and tire scrubbing.
