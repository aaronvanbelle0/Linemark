Frequently Asked Questions — Whatcom County Parking Lot Striping

How often should parking lots in Whatcom County be restriped?

Due to Northwest Washington's wet climate and frequent freeze-thaw cycles, we recommend restriping every 2-3 years for most commercial properties in Bellingham and Whatcom County. High-traffic areas like retail centers along Meridian may need annual restriping to maintain curb appeal and safety.

What are the ADA requirements for parking in Whatcom County?

Washington State requires 1 accessible space for the first 25 parking spots, plus 1 additional space for every 50 spaces thereafter. We ensure full compliance with federal ADA guidelines and Washington State regulations, including proper signage.

Can you stripe parking lots in winter?

Striping requires dry pavement and temperatures above 50°F. In Whatcom County, this typically means April through October. We recommend checking our guide on winterizing your lot for off-season maintenance.

How long does striping take to dry in Whatcom County?

In typical Whatcom County weather conditions, our fast-dry paint sets within 30-45 minutes. We can work during off-hours or section off areas to keep your business operational during the striping process.

Do you serve commercial properties outside Whatcom County?

Yes! We provide parking lot striping throughout all of Whatcom County including Bellingham, Ferndale, Lynden, and Blaine. We also serve Skagit, Snohomish, Island, and San Juan Counties.
