What are the ADA requirements for parking in Whatcom County?

Washington State requires 1 accessible space for the first 25 parking spots, plus 1 additional space for every 50 spaces thereafter. We ensure full compliance with federal ADA guidelines and Washington State regulations, including proper signage.
