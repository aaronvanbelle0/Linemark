Frequently Asked Questions

What types of warehouse markings do you provide?

We provide comprehensive markings including forklift lanes, pedestrian walkways, loading zones, storage area boundaries, safety zones, hazard markings, equipment parking areas, and directional arrows.

Do your markings comply with OSHA standards?

Yes, all our warehouse striping follows OSHA guidelines for industrial facility safety markings, including proper color coding (yellow for caution, red for hazards, etc.) and line widths for visibility.

How long does the paint last in high-traffic areas?

We use heavy-duty industrial-grade epoxy and urethane paints designed to withstand constant forklift traffic, heavy loads, and cleaning procedures. Typical lifespan is 3-5 years depending on usage intensity.

Can you work around our operating schedule?

Yes, we offer flexible scheduling including evening and weekend work to minimize disruption to your operations. Most warehouse striping can be done in sections to keep your facility operational.

What surfaces can you mark?

We work on all warehouse flooring types including sealed concrete, epoxy floors, polished concrete, and other industrial surfaces. We select the appropriate paint system for each surface type.
