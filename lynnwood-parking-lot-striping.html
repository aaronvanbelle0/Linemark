Lynnwood Striping FAQs

Can you stripe at night to avoid mall traffic?

Absolutely. For our Lynnwood retail clients, we frequently perform work between 10 PM and 7 AM to ensure your parking lot is dry and open before the mall or business centers open.

Do you handle curb painting for ADA and fire lanes in Lynnwood?

Yes, we provide full-service curb painting, including safety red for fire lanes and blue for ADA requirements, ensuring your property passes local inspections.

How long will the new lines last in high-traffic areas like Hwy 99?

On high-volume roads like Hwy 99, we use premium high-solids traffic paint. In typical conditions, lines remain bright for 2-3 years, though high-density entrance points may require annual refreshes.
