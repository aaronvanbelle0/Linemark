Everett Striping FAQs

Do you provide warehouse floor markings for Everett industrial sites?

Yes. We provide internal safety markings for warehouses and manufacturing plants in the Everett and Paine Field areas, including forklift lanes, pedestrian zones, and hazard markings. See our Warehouse & Factory Markings service page for full details.

Can you stripe at night to avoid traffic on Broadway?

Absolutely. We understand that Everett is a 24/7 city. We schedule work during the "Ghost Shift" (typically 10 PM to 5 AM) using acetone-based rapid-dry paint with a 5-minute cure time, ensuring zero disruption to your business or industrial operations.

Are your fire lane markings compliant with Everett Fire Marshal codes?

Yes. All fire lane markings are applied to meet the specific requirements of the Everett Fire Department for emergency vehicle access, including correct curb color, width, and "NO PARKING FIRE LANE" stenciling.
