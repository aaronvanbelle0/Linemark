Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our parking lot striping services in Northwest Washington

How long does parking lot striping last?

With proper maintenance, professional parking lot striping typically lasts 1-2 years in the Pacific Northwest. We recommend a 2-year restriping cycle to combat the specific rain and traffic wear typical of our region.

How long until the parking lot can be used after striping?

Drying time depends on weather conditions. In good weather (dry, warm), lines typically dry in 30-60 minutes. We recommend waiting 1-2 hours before allowing vehicle traffic to ensure complete drying and maximum durability.

Do you offer ADA compliant parking space striping?

Yes! We specialize in ADA-compliant parking lot striping including properly sized handicap spaces, van-accessible spaces, access aisles, and international accessibility symbols. We ensure your parking lot meets all federal and Washington State accessibility requirements.

What areas do you serve for parking lot striping?

We provide parking lot striping services throughout Whatcom County including Lynden and Bellingham. We also serve Skagit County, Snohomish County, San Juan County, and Island County. Contact us to confirm service availability for your location.
