Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our athletic field striping services in Northwest Washington

How often do athletic fields need to be restriped?

Most natural turf and synthetic fields need fresh lines before each season, or every 3–6 weeks during active use. Heavy rain, mowing, and foot traffic all accelerate line wear. LineMark recommends a pre-season application plus mid-season touch-ups for school and park facilities in the Pacific Northwest.

Do you stripe on natural grass as well as synthetic turf?

Yes. LineMark stripes on natural grass, synthetic turf, and dirt infields. We select the appropriate aerosol or water-based field marking paint for each surface type — safe for turf root systems and clearly visible from the sidelines and stands.

What field sports does LineMark stripe?

We stripe soccer, football, baseball, softball, lacrosse, field hockey, and flag football fields. We can also lay out multi-sport configurations with color-coded lines for facilities that host several sports on the same turf.

What areas do you serve for field striping?

We provide field striping throughout Whatcom County including Lynden, Bellingham, Ferndale, Blaine, and Everson. We also serve clients in Skagit County, Snohomish County, San Juan County, and Island County.
