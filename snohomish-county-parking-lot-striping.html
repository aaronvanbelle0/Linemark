Frequently Asked Questions — Snohomish County Parking Lot Striping

How often should parking lots in Snohomish County be restriped?

Due to the frequent rain and "Everett Grey" overcast skies, visibility is crucial. We recommend restriping every 2-3 years. To combat low-light conditions, we often recommend adding reflective beads to your traffic paint for superior night-time visibility.

What are the ADA requirements for parking in Snohomish County?

Washington State requires 1 accessible space for the first 25 parking spots, plus 1 additional space for every 50 spaces thereafter. We ensure full compliance with federal ADA guidelines and WA State signage laws.

Can you stripe parking lots in winter?

Striping requires dry pavement and temperatures above 50°F. In Snohomish County, this typically means April through October. We recommend scheduling your project during the drier summer months for optimal paint adhesion and durability.

How long does striping take to dry?

In typical weather conditions, our fast-dry paint sets within 30-45 minutes. For busy locations like the Seattle Premium Outlets or grocery centers, we can section off areas or work overnight to keep your business operational.

Do you serve commercial properties outside Snohomish County?

Yes! We provide parking lot striping throughout Snohomish County and also serve Whatcom, Skagit, Island, and San Juan Counties.
