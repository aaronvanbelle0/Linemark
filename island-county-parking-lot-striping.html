Frequently Asked Questions - Island County Parking Lot Striping

How often should parking lots in Island County be restriped?

Due to the exposure to salt air and heavy rain, we recommend restriping every 2 years. We use specialized paints designed to resist corrosion and fading in coastal environments.

What are the ADA requirements for parking in Island County?

We ensure full compliance with federal ADA guidelines and Washington State regulations. For businesses in historic districts like Coupeville, we can help address slope and grade challenges to ensure accessibility.

Can you stripe parking lots in winter?

Striping requires dry pavement and temperatures above 50°F. In Island County, this typically means April through October. We recommend checking our winter maintenance guide to prepare your lot for the off-season.

Do you serve commercial properties outside Island County?

Yes! We serve all of Whidbey and Camano Islands, as well as Skagit, Snohomish, Whatcom, and San Juan Counties.
