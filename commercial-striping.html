Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our commercial line striping services in Whatcom County

How long does commercial line striping last?

With proper maintenance, professional commercial line striping typically lasts 2-3 years depending on traffic volume. For high-traffic industrial areas with heavy equipment, we recommend thermoplastic markings which can last 5+ years. We use materials specifically designed to withstand Whatcom County's wet climate and heavy use.

How long until the facility can be used after striping?

Drying time depends on weather conditions and materials used. Standard paint typically dries in 30-60 minutes in good weather. Thermoplastic markings cool and harden within 15-20 minutes. We recommend waiting 1-2 hours before allowing heavy equipment traffic to ensure maximum durability.

Do you offer OSHA-compliant safety marking?

Yes! We specialize in OSHA-compliant safety marking including hazard zones, pedestrian walkways, equipment clearance zones, and color-coded safety areas. We ensure your facility meets all federal workplace safety marking requirements and can help design a comprehensive safety marking plan.

What areas do you serve for commercial striping?

We provide commercial line striping services throughout Whatcom County including Lynden, Bellingham, Ferndale, Blaine, Everson, and Nooksack. We also serve parts of Skagit, Snohomish, San Juan, and Island Counties. Contact us to confirm service availability for your location.

How much does commercial line striping cost?

Pricing varies based on facility size, project scope, material requirements, and surface condition. Most commercial and industrial projects range from $1,000-$10,000+ depending on complexity. Contact us for a free, no-obligation quote specific to your facility and requirements.
