Quality Striping Solutions

LineMark Striping is a professional line striping company based in Lynden, Washington. We specialize in parking lot striping, road marking, and commercial line painting services throughout Whatcom County.

With years of experience and a commitment to quality, we provide reliable, professional striping services that meet the highest standards. Our team uses modern equipment and high-quality materials to ensure long-lasting results.
