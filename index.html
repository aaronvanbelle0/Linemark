Quality Striping Solutions

LineMark Striping is a professional line striping company based in Lynden, Washington. We specialize in parking lot striping, road marking, and commercial line painting services throughout Whatcom County.

We take pride in delivering clear, accurate, and professional results on every job. Using modern equipment and high-quality materials, we ensure each project is completed with care, durability, and attention to detail. Licensed, Insured, and Bonded.
