About LineMark Striping

Your trusted partner for professional line striping services

Quality Striping Solutions

LineMark Striping is a professional line striping company based in Lynden, Washington. We specialize in parking lot striping and commercial line painting services throughout Whatcom County. At LineMark Striping, we don't just paint lines; we engineer parking solutions. We specialize in commercial line painting materials that withstand the specific rain and freeze-thaw cycles of Whatcom County. From ADA signage compliance in Ferndale to large-scale layouts in Bellingham, we help property managers reduce liability and improve curb appeal.

We take pride in delivering clear, accurate, and professional results on every job. Using modern equipment and high-quality materials, we ensure each project is completed with care, durability, and attention to detail.
