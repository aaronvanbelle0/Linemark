Blaine Striping FAQs

Does the salt air in Blaine affect the lifespan of the paint?

Yes, coastal environments are tougher on markings. We use high-solids traffic paint and can offer thermoplastic options that are more resistant to salt and moisture.

Absolutely. We often work night shifts for Blaine businesses to ensure we don't interfere with daytime commerce or tourist traffic near the border.
