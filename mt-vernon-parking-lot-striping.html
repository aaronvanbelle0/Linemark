Mt Vernon Striping FAQs

When is the best time to restripe a lot in Mt Vernon?

For the best adhesion and longevity, we recommend striping between May and September when pavement temperatures are stable. We can work around your business hours to minimize disruption.

Do you handle curb painting for ADA and fire lanes?

Yes, we provide full-service curb painting, including blue ADA curbs and safety red fire lanes, ensuring your property is fully compliant with local Skagit County codes.

How long does it take for the paint to dry?

Under normal conditions, our professional traffic paint is dry to the touch in 30-45 minutes. We recommend keeping traffic off the lines for at least 1 hour for the best results.
