Full courts to half courts. Schools to HOAs. New layouts to restripes.

From HOA communities in Lynden and Bellingham to school districts in Mount Vernon and Anacortes to park facilities in Everett and Marysville, LineMark Striping handles basketball court line marking of every scale across Northwest Washington. Whether you need a quick half-court restripe at a community complex or a full regulation layout at a school campus, we deliver crisp, durable lines using Sherwin-Williams commercial paint built to withstand the Pacific Northwest's heavy rainfall and UV exposure.

Basketball courts are also the most common surface we pair with pickleball overlays and tennis line additions to create full multi-sport facilities. If your facility is adding any court markings, doing everything at once saves time and cost.

Full Court Restriping

Restore faded or worn basketball court lines to regulation standards. We verify measurements, re-mask, and apply a fresh 2-coat commercial paint system. All lines included — key, arc, center circle, half-court, and boundaries.

Half Court Striping

Half courts are the most popular choice for HOAs, apartment complexes, and community parks. We stripe standard recreational half courts or regulation-dimension layouts depending on your slab size and use case.

New Court Layout

New slab ready for marking? LineMark coordinates with your contractor to stripe basketball courts at the correct phase. We handle layout planning, precise measurement, masking, and commercial paint application.

Multi-Sport Overlays

Add pickleball or tennis lines to an existing basketball court using color-coded lines. Each sport stays clearly distinguishable. This is the most cost-effective way to maximize any court slab in Northwest Washington.

School & Parks Projects

Basketball Court Sizes — What's Right for Your Facility?

LineMark stripes every court size from residential half courts to full regulation layouts.

Choosing the right court size depends on your available slab space, your facility's users, and your budget. Most HOAs and parks in Whatcom County and Skagit County opt for recreational half courts — they require roughly half the space and cost of a full court while still delivering an excellent playing experience. Schools and larger parks typically go full court.

NBA / NCAA Full Court

94 × 50 ft

Regulation full court used by professional and college programs. Includes all standard lines. Best for schools, athletic clubs, and large recreation centers.

High School Full Court

84 × 50 ft

Standard for WIAA high school competition in Washington State. Common for school campuses across Whatcom, Skagit, and Snohomish Counties.

Regulation Half Court

47 × 50 ft

Half of a full NBA court. Includes key, three-point arc, free throw line, and half-court line. Popular for parks and larger HOA facilities.

Recreational Half Court

30–42 × 50 ft

The most common choice for HOA communities, apartment complexes, and small parks. Maximizes play space on a compact slab. LineMark customizes to your exact dimensions.

3-on-3 Court

45 × 49 ft

FIBA 3x3 regulation layout. Increasingly popular for community tournaments and youth programs across Northwest Washington parks.

Multi-Sport Layout

Custom

Basketball lines combined with pickleball or tennis markings on a single slab. Color-coded lines keep each sport clearly distinguishable. Most cost-effective court upgrade available.
